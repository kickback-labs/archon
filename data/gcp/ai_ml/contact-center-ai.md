## GCP Contact Center AI (CCAI)

### Description
Google Cloud Contact Center AI (CCAI) is a suite of AI-powered products and capabilities that help contact centers modernize their customer service operations through intelligent automation and agent assistance. CCAI encompasses multiple integrated products: Conversational Agents (virtual agents that handle customer interactions end-to-end), Agent Assist (real-time guidance and suggestions for human agents), and Conversational Insights (post-call analytics). The platform is also available as a fully managed, omnichannel Contact Center as a Service (CCaaS) offering — branded as Customer Engagement Suite — that includes native telephony (VoIP via WebRTC and PSTN), chat, SMS, email, and social channels in a single platform. CCAI integrates with popular CRMs such as Salesforce, Microsoft Dynamics, and Zendesk, and supports visual IVR, AI-driven predictive routing, and workforce management. Sensitive data handling and compliance features are built in using Google Cloud's security and privacy foundations.

### Use Cases
* AI-powered self-service virtual agents to deflect routine inbound calls and chats without human intervention (e.g., account balance inquiries, appointment scheduling)
* Real-time agent assist providing suggested responses, next-best-action guidance, and automatic knowledge base lookups during live customer interactions
* Post-call transcript analysis and sentiment scoring to identify coaching opportunities and surface operational trends (e.g., trending complaint topics)
* Omnichannel contact center deployment with a single agent desktop spanning voice, chat, email, and social channels with persistent conversation context
* AI-driven predictive routing to match customers with the most suitable available agent based on intent, history, and agent skill profile
* Visual IVR for web and mobile apps that replaces traditional audio IVR with a graphical, self-service flow to reduce handle times and abandonment rates
* Integration of Conversational Agents and Agent Assist into an existing telephony infrastructure without full platform migration
* Workforce and quality management including call recording, agent scheduling, adherence tracking, and performance dashboards
